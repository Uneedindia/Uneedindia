The Unique You Need
that's what UneedIndia stands for. Growing up, our mothers and grandmothers would knit and create things thoughtfully, customizing every detail just for us.
That love became the inspiration behind UneedIndia, and we wanted to bring that same feeling to everyone.
Today, we are a team of passionate women from across India, working towards that vision.
Two years in, we are incredibly grateful for the trust of our customers and the growing community that continues to support us.
We can't wait to create more, deliver more, and pass on the same love that the women in our lives once shared with us through their handmade creations. Alongside UneedIndia, we also run Choice Hub, our offline gifting store in Udaipur, Rajasthan, where thoughtful gifting has always been at the heart of what we do. Do check it out!
